Kai Yi

I’m a junior student at Xi’an Jiaotong University in China, major in software engineering. Now I’m a research intern at Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics and National Engineering Laboratory for Visual Information Processing and Applications in Xi’an Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Nanning Zheng and Dr. Shitao Chen. My research interest is cognitive science, computer vision, machine learning, chaos and fractal. I’m thinking and designing the proper method of using cognitive mechanisms (e.g., attention, memory, learning, knowledge) to build more dynamic, robust and intelligent systems. Looking forward to be a PHD candidate in an ideal university.
